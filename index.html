Why the Horrible school life?

School life is supposed to be fun and exciting, but sometimes it feels more like a survival game. From waking up at terrible hours to endless exams, every day brings a new challenge that makes students sigh loudly.

Instead of adventure and joy, the dreaded school life gives us mountains of homework, surprise exams, and boring lectures. Students become professional warriors against stress, fatigue, and poor grades.

Everyone talks about “the best years of your life,” but let’s be honest—sometimes school life is more like a comedy horror movie. You laugh, you cry, you panic, and somehow you still have to wake up and do it all again tomorrow.
